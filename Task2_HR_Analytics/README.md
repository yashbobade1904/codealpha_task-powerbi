# Task 2: Human Resources Analytics Dashboard

## Objective
Analyze and optimize HR processes and workforce management.

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Total Employees, Attrition, Avg Salary, Satisfaction)
- Attrition Rate (Donut Chart)
- Attrition by Department (Bar Chart)
- Avg Salary by Job Role (Bar Chart)
- Attrition by Age Group (Column Chart)
- Attrition Trend by Years (Line Chart)
- OverTime by Department (Stacked Bar)
- Interactive Slicers (Department & Gender)

## Key Insights
- Overall Attrition Rate: 13.84%
- Total Employees: 961
- Avg Monthly Salary: 6.28K

## Tool Used
Power BI Desktop

## Dataset
IBM HR Employee Attrition Dataset (Kaggle)
